03 — Content Buckets

Instagram Content Bucket List

🎓 Education & Insights
Teach your audience something actionable about digital marketing. Break down SEO concepts, explain how Google Ads work, or demystify Meta's algorithm — all through the lens of the UAE market. Posts like "Why your Google Ads cost more in Dubai" or a step-by-step campaign setup guide build long-term authority and get saved repeatedly.
3× / week · Carousel
📊 Data & Stats
Lead with a bold number that stops the scroll, then give it context. The UAE digital ad market is worth AED 18B — that's a hook. Stats like "68% of online experiences start with search" or Gulf-specific ROAS benchmarks make followers stop, screenshot, and share. One stat, one insight, one strong visual is all you need.
2× / week · Single Image
🏆 Case Studies & Results
Nothing converts a potential client faster than proof. Show the before and after — CPL dropped from AED 80 to AED 30, a Dubai F&B brand going from AED 5K to AED 30K ROAS, or a local brand climbing from Page 7 to Page 1 in 90 days. Anonymised or consented, real numbers tell the story no caption can.
1–2× / week · Carousel
🔧 Tools & Resources
Curated tool and resource posts are among the most saved content type on Instagram. Round up the best free SEO tools, recommend landing page builders that support Arabic and English, or share Google Ads scripts that save hours of manual work. These posts position the agency as genuinely helpful — not just promotional.
1× / week · Carousel
🧠 Thought Leadership
Share opinions, not just information. Push back on common myths — "Most UAE brands don't need more traffic, they need better conversion" is a post that sparks debate and earns followers. Talk about what managing AED 1M in ad spend actually taught you, or make bold predictions about where MENA digital marketing is heading in 2026 and beyond.
2× / week · Reel
😄 Relatable & Humor
Humor is the fastest way to get shared by people who've never heard of you. Lean into the everyday frustrations of agency life — clients wanting to go viral on an AED 500 budget, explaining SEO to someone who just wants to rank #1 by tomorrow, or the chaos of a campaign launch day. If marketers in Dubai see themselves in the post, they'll tag someone.
2× / week · Single Image
🌟 Behind the Scenes
People don't just hire agencies — they hire people. BTS content humanises the brand and makes it feel real. Show how a campaign goes from client brief to live ads, introduce the team, or capture that unscripted moment when a campaign launches and everyone's watching the dashboard refresh. Authenticity here builds more trust than any polished post.
2× / week · Reel + Story
📣 Brand & Services
Keep service posts benefit-led, not feature-led. Instead of listing what the agency does, show what the client gets — lower CPL, higher ROAS, more qualified leads. A free Google Ads audit CTA in Stories, a carousel breaking down a performance marketing package in AED, or a post on why Dubai brands switch agencies are all strong ways to drive inbound without being pushy.
1× / week · Carousel

04 — Format Guide

Post Formats & Best Practices

🎠 Carousels95%
7–10 slides. Hook on slide 1–2. End with a save / share CTA.
🎬 Reels90%
Under 30 sec. Hook in first 2 seconds. Add captions — most watch without sound.
📱 Stories78%
3–5/day. Use polls, questions & CTAs. Best format for DMs & leads.
🖼️ Single Image62%
Best for memes, stat posts, quotes. High shares, lower saves.
🇦🇪 Dubai-Specific Tips

Best posting times: 8–10 AM GST and 7–9 PM GST. Friday is the highest-engagement day in UAE. Bilingual captions (English + Arabic keywords) improve local Explore discoverability. Ramadan content spikes 35% in MENA — plan 2 weeks ahead.

📅 UAE Work Week

The UAE week runs Sunday–Thursday. Post high-effort content (carousels, case studies) on Sunday and Tuesday when professional engagement peaks. Friday & Saturday — personal browsing hours — are ideal for BTS and humor posts.
